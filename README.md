## 🚨 Emergency first

If someone may be overdosing right now, call **000** immediately.
Paramedics carry naloxone and are often the fastest path to help.

---

## ✨ What NyxDrop does

NyxDrop helps people find places that may provide take-home naloxone, then adds the community context official lists often miss.

It is designed for people who are stressed, time-poor, or tired of being judged at the counter.

### What you can see at a glance

- **Recent outcomes**: whether people recently got naloxone there
- **Barrier signals**: reports of ID requests, Medicare requests, cost, long waits, or difficult interactions
- **Practical details**: directions, opening hours, phone number, and naloxone form

---

## 💛 How it helps

| Need | What NyxDrop gives you |
|---|---|
| Planning ahead | A map of options near you |
| Fast decisions | Recent, community-reported experiences |
| Lower friction | Filters that help avoid recently flagged barriers |
| Helping others | Anonymous visit reports that support the next person |

---

## 🧭 How to use it

1. Open the map and search by suburb, address, or place name.
2. Tap a pin to view details and recent reports.
3. Use filters to hide places where common barriers were recently reported.
4. Save reliable locations in **My Places**.
5. After visiting, submit a short anonymous report.

---

## 🔄 Two modes, one map

### 🗺️ Plan mode
For everyday planning:
- full map view
- filters and richer details
- save/watch trusted spots

### ⚡ Now mode
For urgent moments:
- prominent **Call 000** action
- simplified map for quick decisions
- quick directions and call buttons
- built-in DRSABCD reminder card

---

## 🔒 Privacy by design

NyxDrop is built to protect community users:

- no public account required
- anonymous reporting
- random device key stored locally (not personal identity)
- one-tap **Forget this device** option

---

## ♿ Community accessible

NyxDrop aims to stay accessible in real-world conditions:

- mobile-first interface
- high-contrast and reduced-motion options
- multilingual support (community-reviewed)

---

## 🤝 Community trust model

NyxDrop combines four signals so people can make safer decisions before arriving:

- **Recency** (what happened recently)
- **Reliability** (longer-term pattern)
- **Verification** (community/official confidence)
- **Guardian notes** (trusted community partners)

---

## 🇦🇺 About naloxone access in Australia

Take Home Naloxone (THN) supports access without prescription, but real-world experiences still vary place to place.
NyxDrop exists to surface that lived reality through anonymous community reporting.

---

## 📌 Important

NyxDrop supports overdose-prevention planning and community knowledge sharing.
It does **not** replace emergency services.

In an emergency, call **000**.
